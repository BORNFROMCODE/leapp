`leapp team`
============

Login to your Team account

* [`leapp team login`](#leapp-team-login)
* [`leapp team logout`](#leapp-team-logout)

## `leapp team login`

Login to your Team account

```
USAGE
  $ leapp team login

DESCRIPTION
  Login to your Team account

EXAMPLES
  $leapp team login
```

## `leapp team logout`

Logout from your Team account

```
USAGE
  $ leapp team logout

DESCRIPTION
  Logout from your Team account

EXAMPLES
  $leapp team logout
```
